Example normal report

Findings:

Normal liver morphology and enhancement. No masses. Normal gallbladder morphology. Normal calibre intrahepatic and common bile ducts.

Normal pancreatic morphology and enhancement. No ductal dilation or masses. No peripancreatic fluid.

Normal spleen and adrenal glands.

Kidneys are normal in size and enhancement. No focal renal lesions or hydronephrosis. No urinary tract calculi. Normal appearances of the bladder.

Normal appearances of the small and large bowel. Normal appendix

No abdominopelvic ascites or extraluminal gas

Portal, splenic, and superior mesenteric veins are patent. Visceral arteries are patent. No abdominal aortic aneurysm.

No enlarged abdominopelvic lymph nodes

The imaged lung bases are clear.

No fractures or aggressive focal bone lesions.

Opinion:

Normal appearances of the abdomen and pelvis
